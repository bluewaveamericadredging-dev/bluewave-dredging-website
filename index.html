Who We Are

Delivering Precision Dredging with Strength, Scale, and Innovation

BlueWave America Dredging Inc. is a privately held, multi-disciplinary dredging and marine construction company offering a full portfolio of services spanning heavy civil construction, environmental dredging, beach renourishment, navigation improvement, and equipment leasing.

Comprehensive Dredging and Marine Construction Solutions

Coastal Protection

Our Approach

Data-Driven Execution with a Commitment to Sustainability

Our approach integrates advanced engineering, real-time monitoring, and strict environmental compliance to ensure safe and effective project delivery. By combining technology with operational expertise, BlueWave minimizes ecological impact while maximizing efficiency and value for every client.

Our Capabilities

Advanced Equipment and Scalable Operations

BlueWave operates a modern fleet of cutter suction dredgers supported by high-capacity booster stations, floating and submerged pipeline systems, and specialized barge operations. This integrated capability enables us to efficiently execute projects ranging from inland waterways to large-scale coastal and offshore developments.
