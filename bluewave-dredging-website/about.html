About BlueWave America Dredging Inc.

Engineering the Depths. Advancing What Matters Above.

BlueWave America Dredging Inc. is a New York–based marine engineering and hydraulic dredging company specializing in river restoration, port deepening, coastal protection, canal excavation, and land reclamation projects.

Headquartered in Hollis, NY, we deliver turnkey dredging solutions through integrated operations that include cutter suction dredgers, booster stations, floating pipeline systems, and barge support—built for precision, performance, and scale.

Who We Are

A Modern Marine Construction Partner Built for Complex Environments

BlueWave was built around a simple principle: critical marine infrastructure demands more than equipment alone—it requires disciplined execution, engineering depth, environmental awareness, and a commitment to long-term value.

Our work spans the restoration and enhancement of waterways, ports, shorelines, and reclaimed land. We bring together hydraulic dredging capability, field logistics, and operational coordination to help clients move from concept to delivery with confidence.

Whether supporting navigation improvement, sediment management, coastal resilience, or marine development, BlueWave approaches every project with precision, safety, and a performance-driven mindset.
